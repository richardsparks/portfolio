The Guardian

Careers website

Team lead, UX design & direction

The brief

Guardian News & Media were going through a big change with their main site, updating to a new design, platform and URL. They required a similar update to their careers site attract candidates who believed in their values.

The audience

We indentifed seven distinct personas across their core divisions, each with their own specific needs.

Journalist

Agile Project Manager

Junior Client Side Developer

Account Manager Advertising Sales

Management Acountant

Sub-Editor

Graduate

User testing

One of the main problems identifed with the exsisting site was its visibilty, users were often getting lost and repeatedly failing to find opportunities available.

Our mission

To produce a website that is unque to recruitment yet dovetailed perfectly with the beta site.

To differentiate Guardian News & Media by showcasing their unique values and philosophy.

To make sure everything was super easy to find.

Simplification

We made a series of UX recommendations including clear signposting, limiting cross linking with Guardian jobs and making it easier to filter jobs.

We also significated reduced the page count from the previous site.

Our positioning

It's selling a place of work, not just providing information. It should refelct the business ethos, not just be about content.
