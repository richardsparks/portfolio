Allen & Overy

Graduate careers website

Design & UX direction

The brief

To secure the best talent and maintain their position as a leading law firm, Allen & Overy asked us to to re-launch the UK graduate website.

Our audience

Our target audience are driven students on the cusp of something great and Allen & Overy can provide a springboard to the challenges, recognition and rewards they deserve.

Defining objectives

We started with a content audit of the existing site, re-architeching the information, producing new content and a complete re-design of the overall look a feel.

Prototype

Through our audience insights we produced and tested a number of website prototypes. This enabled us to refine the user experience so that it truly reflected our audience needs.

Design

Before any new adventure there is a sense of excitement and we wanted to convey that feeling on the website homepage. Through careful use of animation and video we create a sense of wonder and invite the user to begin their journey with us.

Campaign update

As part of a recent refresh we updated a number of features on the site including a new interactive homepage image which utilises advanced 3D shaders to give the illusion of moving water.

"If the standard of applications we receive match the quality of the look and feel of the new site, then I think the graduate recruitment team are in for a very successful year."

Chris Rice - Global Recruiting Systems Manager
